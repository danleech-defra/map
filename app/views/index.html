How to draw a shape
  1. Adjust the map until your area is in view
  2. Press 'Start drawing'
  3. Choose a starting point on your shape and adjust the map to move this into the circle
  4. Press 'confirm point'
  5. Repeat step 3 to 4 until you have marked all the edges of your shape
  6. Press 'finish shape'
