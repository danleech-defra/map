How to draw a shape

Mobile phone or tablet

  1. Adjust the map until your area is in view
  2. Press 'Start drawing'
  3. Choose a starting point for your shape and adjust the map to move this into the circle
  4. Press 'confirm point'
  5. Repeat step 3 to 4 until you have marked all the edges of your shape
  6. Press 'finish shape'

Using a mouse

  1. Adjust the map until your area is in view
  2. Press 'Start drawing'
  3. Choose a starting point for your shape and click to place a point
  4. Repeat step 3 until you have marked all the edges of your shape
  5. Click on the first point again to finish
